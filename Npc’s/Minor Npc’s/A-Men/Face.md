Bard

Can convince another creature to agree that a favor was done for them for calling in a favor.

Calling in a favor: As an action, this creature may temporarily "borrow" another creatures cursed technique. While borrowed the original creature may not use their cursed technique.

To call in a favor it is a two-step process
1. Know the name of the ability or see it being used.
2. This creature must have done the owner a favor and have the owner agree a favor was done.
